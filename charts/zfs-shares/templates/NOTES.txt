NOTE ON UPGRADES: the ZfsShare and ZfsPool CRDs are installed from the chart's
crds/ directory. `helm upgrade` does NOT update them (no Helm flag does). After
upgrading to a release that changes a CRD schema, apply the CRDs manually first:

     kubectl apply -f charts/zfs-shares/crds/     # local checkout; or `make install-crd`
     # published chart: helm pull ... --untar && kubectl apply -f zfs-shares/crds/
